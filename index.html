What is NSS

National Service Scheme

A Central Sector Scheme of Government of India, Ministry of Youth Affairs & Sports.

NSS
NOT ME, BUT YOU
Official NSS Motto
Inspired by Swami Vivekananda's thoughts, this motto expresses the essence of democratic living and upholds the need for selfless service.
🎯

Our Mission

To develop the personality and character of the student youth through voluntary community service, integrating the students with the local community.

🌍

Community Service

NSS volunteers undertake a minimum of 120 hours of social work over two years, including a Special Camp of 7 days in adopted villages.

📜

Government Recognition

NSS certificates are recognized by UPSC and various state public service commissions, giving bonus marks during recruitment processes.

🏛️

GIOE NSS Unit

Our unit at Government Institute of Electronics, Hyderabad runs under the Telangana State NSS Cell with 120+ active volunteers.

Why Join NSS

Benefits of Joining NSS

NSS membership opens doors beyond the classroom — academically, professionally, and personally.

01

UPSC & Govt Job Bonus

NSS certificate holders receive priority and bonus marks in UPSC, SSC, and Telangana state government recruitment processes.

02

Republic Day Camp

Top performers get selected for the prestigious National Republic Day Camp in New Delhi, a life-changing experience.

03

Leadership Development

Take charge of real programs, manage teams, and build soft skills that set you apart in any career path you choose.

04

Scholarship Eligibility

NSS volunteers are eligible for various state and national scholarships, including those for rural development work.

05

Certificate of Merit

On completion of 2 years, receive an official NSS Certificate signed by the Programme Officer — recognized across India.

06

Network & Exposure

Connect with NSS volunteers across Telangana, participate in district and state-level events, and broaden your horizons.
